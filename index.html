Liveability Score Methodology

Our liveability scores combine multiple NASA Earth observation datasets to provide comprehensive environmental assessments:

30%
Air Quality
NO2 levels, aerosol optical depth from AIRS/OMI satellites
25%
Water Resources
Precipitation patterns (GPM) and groundwater levels (GRACE)
20%
Vegetation Health
NDVI from MODIS/Landsat indicating green spaces
15%
Temperature
Land surface temperature from MODIS thermal bands

Score Calculation

1. Normalize each indicator (0-1)

2. Apply weighted average

3. Scale to 0-100 range

NASA Earth Observation Data Sources

Satellite Missions
  • MODIS - Vegetation health (NDVI), land surface temperature
  • Landsat - High-resolution land cover and vegetation analysis
  • AIRS/OMI - Atmospheric composition and air quality monitoring
  • GPM - Global precipitation measurement
  • GRACE - Groundwater and water storage changes
Data Processing
Demo Mode Active: This platform uses bundled JSON data for demonstration. Production version integrates with NASA's Global Imagery Browse Services (GIBS) and Earth Data APIs for real-time satellite imagery and environmental data.

Data is processed through NASA's Earth Science Data Systems (ESDS) and delivered via standardized APIs with global coverage and regular updates.
